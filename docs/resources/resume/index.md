# Resume and CV Building, Values Exercises, and Material Preparation

* [Drafting a Personal Mission Statement](assets/pdfs/personal_statement.pdf)
This exercise from the Nonprofit Academy of Wisconsin guides you through creating a mission statement you can use on a resume and to shape how you pitch yourself in an interview. It draws on personal anecdotes and associated values/causes.
